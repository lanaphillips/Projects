Happy Go Lucky Pet Care™ is a professional, owner operated, in-home pet care service. We provide professional dog walking and pet sitting. I'm sorry but we do not provide boarding. We provide warm, loving and reliable pet care in the comfort of your pets familiar environment. We care for pet's in Walnut Creek, Lafayette, Alamo and Pleasant Hill. We are licensed, bonded, and insured for your protection. We are Pet First Aid and CPR Certified through the American Red Cross.

We understand that choosing the right pet and home care service is one of the most important decisions you will make for your pet. We pay attention to the details. We treat your pet's as if they were our own.

Let Happy Go Lucky Pet Care™ provide you with the peace of mind that you deserve while you're away from your pet. We ensure that your pet will get the proper nurturing in a familiar environment and the skilled training that they deserve.

We provide service for the following cities: Walnut Creek, Lafayette, Alamo and Pleasant Hill.
