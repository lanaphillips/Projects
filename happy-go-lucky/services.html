Services

  • Dog Walking

    You will come home to a more relaxed and content pet. The daily walk is the perfect solution for the busy owner. We tailor the pace of the walk to your dogs specific needs and ability to ensure they get a sufficient amount of exercise during each visit. We pay attention to the details. Your dogs paws will be clean after a muddy walk on a rainy day. They will be kept cool during the heat of the summer.

    Total visit is at least 30 minutes.

  • Daily Pet Sitting Visit

    We will come to your pets home to care for them while you are away giving you peace of mind. We will duplicate your routine with your pet. This will ensure your pets are as comfortable as possible during your absence. Each visit is customized to your pets individual needs and may include:

    • Fresh water and feeding
    • Administering medications
    • Personalized attention and play time
    • Cleanup of cat litter box and accidents
    • Mail & newspaper pick-up
    • Light plant watering
    • Home Security

    Total visit is at least 30 minutes.

  • Puppy Visiting

    Visits include walking, feeding and play to ensure your puppy feels safe and secure. A special focus is on reinforcing the house-training and basic commands that you are working on. This can Include paper-training, cleaning puppy accidents and redirecting chewing. When your puppy is current on all shots we can provide healthy socialization with other dogs.

    Total visit is at least 30 minutes.

  • Small & Furry Critters

    House Rabbits, Guinea Pigs, Hamsters, Domestic Rats, etc. Includes food and water change, cage maintenance, love and cuddles.

    Total visit is at least 30 minutes.

  • Feathered Friends

    Includes food and water change, cage or perch stand maintenance, love and attention.

    Total visit is at least 30 minutes.

  • Errand Service

    We can help manage your pet care responsibilities. Our pet errand service can include the following:

    • Accompanying your pet to a veterinary appointment
    • Communicating your pets needs to their veterinarian
    • Drop off and pick-up for grooming appointments
    • Purchasing pet food, medications and supplies from local pet stores or veterinary offices

    One hour minimum charge.
