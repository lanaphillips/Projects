ASCAS


ASCAS is a non profit organisation. We are in the process of applying for charitable status, but in the meantime, any profits will be donated to the charities shown on the “we support” page.

The aim of ASCAS is to bring positive changes to the cosmetic products sold in the Pet Industry. At present there are no regulations for non-pharmaceutical animal shampoos and cosmetics. This means that anything can be put in a bottle and sold to pet owners. There doesn’t have to be an ingredients list, a best before date or any information that shows it is fit for purpose.

As well as campaigning for the laws on this to be changed, ASCAS has provided a certification. ASCAS has 3 types of certification, a one star, two star and three star. This means an ACSAS certification is achievable for all manufacturers at different levels.

A one star certification approves products that are at a standard that would allow them to be sold in the market as cosmetic personal care products for humans. A product that has a two star certification will be milder and kinder to the skin as there are ingredients that are restricted to achieve this rating. The aim of the three star certification is to approve products that are natural and good for the environment.

Co-founder Jane Cooper says “Having been in Pet Retail for years I know that consumers want to be informed about the products they are buying and using. We are constantly asked whether the products we sell are natural, what ingredients are in them and if they have a best before date. I’d say a good 90% of the products on the market do not have a full ingredients list and it makes it very hard to give consumers the answers they are looking for. I have now developed our own range of natural shampoos and cosmetics WildWash that have achieved a 3 star ASCAS certification and I hope this will encourage other manufacturers to do the same.”

ASCAS work with a fully qualified cosmetic chemist who has 10 years experience working in the Cosmetics Industry. Our chemist is educated to degree level and has the SCS Diploma in Cosmetic Science. He has experience in all sectors of the Cosmetics Industry; product development, manufacturing and ingredient suppliers. He has a wide experience across all market sectors, though is a specialist in formulating products for the Natural Cosmetics market.
