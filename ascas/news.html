January 1 - 2013

Wildwash is the first UK company to achieve an ASCAS 3 star certification.

This completely natural range includes pet shampoos, conditioners and spritzes as well as a revolutionary detangler and matt breaker. The range is handmade in a renowned boutique apothecary which focuses on sustainability and concerns for the environment. WildWash is groundbreaking in the pet industry for the quality of ingredients and the research and attention to detail used in all their products. www.wildwash.co.uk

May 10 - 2013

We were invited to become an endorsing organization for The Safe Cosmetics Organisation. This was the wonderful email they sent us!

“Thanks so much for all you're doing to raise awareness about the issue of product safety for our four-legged friends! All the concerns you bring up are so important. Though our work focuses on cosmetics safety for women, men, and children, it looks like the chemicals of concern in animal grooming products that you list on your website are nearly identical to the ones used in children's and adult personal care products. 

I invite you to join our Safe Cosmetics Business Network, which is made up of cosmetics manufacturers, retailers, salon owners and workers, and other members of the industry. Through the Business Network, we work with companies dedicated to personal care product safety to educate more people about the need for improved safety. If you are interested, you can sign on at www.safecosmetics.org/business.

Together, we're shifting the market to safer production for everyone!”

June 15 - 2013

The RSPCA responded to our request for help campaigning “We certainly share your concerns but unfortunately there are a very large number of animal welfare issues requiring attention and we cannot be active on all of them at any one time.

However, your concerns have been noted and we will file your enquiry so that if, at some point in the future, the issues you raise become the subject of wider animal welfare interest and public awareness, we will investigate the matter in greater detail and publish the results.”

Please help us spread the word and create public awareness. Email info@asaca.org for more information.

September 13 - 2013

ASCAS has been named as a Pet Advocate for The Pet Welfare Alliance. It is an amazing organization that brings together an international community of people to act as a watchdog for the poorly legislated multi-billion pet products industry. It comprises pet owners and veterinarians from around the world who join together in strength to take practical, positive action to protect the wellbeing of pets. It expands awareness and understanding of the multi-billion international pet products industry, and the harm that is being done to pets. It raises awareness within the pet owning community to effect positive change on behalf of our pets. Please have a look at their website and join their supporters list. www.petwelfarealliance.org
