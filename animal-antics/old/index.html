Welcome to animal antics

Our mission at Animal Antics is to promote a holistic approach to good pet health, embracing the mind and body of our animal companions through all-natural organic nutrition, alternative health treatments, and fostering well-being through enhanced human-pet bonds.

We empower and educate you to use a natural, nutritious diet along with exercise in play-based obedience training to nurture wellness and ideal health for your pet.

Our facility specializes in offering:

  • Carefully selected holistic, organic and eco-friendly pet foods and products.
  • Full-service grooming and pampering for dogs and cats.
  • Obedience basics, foundation skills and advanced behaviors training in our enriching playroom.
