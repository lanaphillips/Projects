Training

The Animal Antics training philosophy is part of our holistic focus on good mental and physical health for pets. We believe that by showing animals the same love and respect that they freely give us, we establish healthy bonds important to successful training. By sharing with you our knowledge about communication and the wonders of animal behavior, we help you to develop a pet relationship that is a source of joy, and based on trust.

Training should be fun for both human and animal learners, should communicate mutual respect, and should inspire the learning process. Our gentle, cooperative approach teaches socialization skills, modifies problem behaviors, and is the basis of our full spectrum of obedience classes.

All of our classes are taught using Clicker Training. This method is quick, effective, hands-free, force-free, and easy to use for all family members. Clicker training also produces better, longer-lasting results and enthusiastic learners. Best of all, your pet will love to work with you!

Clicker Training for Desired Behavior - Simple, straightforward, and easy to use

Clicker Training is a method that uses a 'marker signal' to tell an animal when they have accomplished the desired behavior. By pairing the behavior with a 'click,' and then following up with something the animal wants (treats, toys, petting, play), the animal will learn to repeat actions that produce enjoyable results.

Traditional training focuses on suppressing bad behavior. Clicker Training is different - the trainer focuses on rewarding good behavior, without using force or fear. Through cooperation, respect, and fun, Clicker Training deepens and enhances our relationships with our pets.

Clicker Training produces creativity, accelerates learning, and creates enthusiastic learners because the methodology stimulates your pet's thinking and relies on your pet interacting with you!

Our Classes

Class goals are:

  • Learn the fundamentals of Clicker Training.
  • Build a training relationship based in fun and love of learning.
  • Eliminate problem behaviors.
  • Establish effective, life-long communication with your pet.

Contact us for a class schedule:

Prestigious Puppies

(For puppies aged 7-16 weeks)
4 weekly classes, one hour. Start your puppy, and you!, on the road to healthy socialization and training practices.

Canine Connection

(For dogs over 4 months old)
8 weekly classes, one hour. Teach your dog foundation skills: sit, down, come when called, leave it, polite leash walking, and much more.

Petite Pals

8 weekly classes, one hour. Follows the curriculum of Canine Connection, but limited to dogs with a breed expectation to remain under 25 pounds.

Canine Connection Too

8 weekly classes, one hour. Build on basic skills to increase your dog's responsiveness and control, even off leash. Participants completing this course qualify for the American Kennel Club (AKC) Canine Good Citizen Test. (Prerequisite: Canine Connection.)

Rally Obedience

Rally-O is a new competitive sport that practices foundation skills while increasing your dog's repertoire of behaviors. (Prerequisite: Canine Connection.)

Classy Cats (kittens 7-14 weeks)

3 weekly classes, one hour. Yes, cats can be trained! Start your kitten on the road to healthy socialization and training practices. Like other social animals, cats need early exposure to new and different people, other animals and new environments. Learn the basics of cat behavior, grooming and health care.

Rock 'N Recall

Every dog should have a reliable recall. This course will introduce the skills necessary to train your dog to come back to you whenever called, under any circumstance. Class sessions will meet at various locations. (Prerequisite: Canine Connection)

Click & Play

Learn advanced skills in animal training while building your relationship with your dog. Dogs will develop creativity and a love of learning. For anyone who wants to teach - and play! - games. (Prerequisite: Canine Connection)

Exceptional Dogs with Special Needs

These classes are for reactive dogs and dogs with aggression issues. Some problem behaviors do arise from insufficient socialization and training, but most arise out of fear and anxiety. This course is designed to give you the skills to modify your dog's behavior, learn to communicate with your dog, and create a long lasting partnership. Private consultations are also available.

Animal Answers

Private Behavior Consultations and Training. For families that are very busy or have scheduling conflicts, we offer private obedience training in your home or at our facility. These sessions are tailored to meet your specific needs.
