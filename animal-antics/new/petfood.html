Pet Foods

Animal Antics' holistic approach to pet health recognizes that our pets look better, feel better and learn better on a nutritionally dense diet optimized for their genetics and lifestyle. Raw foods meet this goal and are the best defense against health problems, allergies and disease.

The raw food movement for pets began in the early 1980s when veterinarians and pet owners began to reconsider the lifetime impact of our pets eating processed foods. Just like for humans, eating a diet of fresh, "real food" is best for animals.

A fresh, natural food diet - fresh, raw proteins, including bones and organ meat, and probiotics such as yogurt - is a cornerstone of holistic pet wellness, for better physiological development to best meet the needs of different types pets at the different stages of their lives.

Why Not Commercial Pet Food?

Commercial pet food first appeared in the United States at the end of the nineteenth century. Within 75 years, the multibillion dollar pet food industry had convinced American pet owners and veterinarians that "scientifically formulated" dog food and cat food was superior nutrition and even defense against disease.

Processed packaged food - full of synthetics, dyes, by-products, preservatives and artificial flavorings - was touted as better for pets than even our own food, including butcher scraps, and became widely accepted as a "nutritional" diet.
