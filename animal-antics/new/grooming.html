Grooming

Our pets deserve the health benefits of a pampered beauty experience and we have designed our service and facility around that mission. Our professional groomers are skilled and loving, use only natural bath products, and rely on gentle, proven techniques.

Whether we are gently introducing your puppy to the grooming experience, offering bath service for smooth coated pets, or maintaining long coats with premier haircutting, our experienced grooming professionals take pride in providing superior service during an enjoyable stay for all pet sizes and breeds.

Your pet's visit begins with a stylist consultation in our clean, relaxing spa environment. We offer full haircutting service, or simple face, paw and sanitary trims when a full haircut is not needed.

A soothing hand-massage bath with high quality, alcohol-free, dye-free and sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners is followed by a personal hand blow-dry. We end with a luxurious brushing and fluff for a calm, pampered pet with a "salon finish" every time.

Ask about our add-on aromatic hypoallergenic shampoos and conditioners for special skin or coat management.

Full Spa Treatment includes:

  • Consultation
  • Complimentary flea and tick inspection
  • Hand massage bath, hand blow-dry and fluff
  • Ear cleaning and plucking
  • Nail trimming and filing
  • Removal of small mats
  • Professional haircutting
  • Luxury brush out
  • Dental cleaning (non-anesthetic)
  • Fresh finishing spray
  • Bow or bandanna
